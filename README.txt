There are 4 objects being rendered in a 3d room.
In the center in a mirror that renders a reflection.
There are also snow particles floating around the scene.

Clicking on one of the objects makes you control them with the movement controls.
Clicking the mouse on the blank space resumes control to the camera.

===MOVEMENT CONTROLS===
WASD - Movement
Numpad 4862 - Camera
Shift - Down
Space - Up

===LIGHTING CONTROLS===
On/Off Keys
0/p - Ambient
9/o - Point
8/i - Directional
7u - Spot
